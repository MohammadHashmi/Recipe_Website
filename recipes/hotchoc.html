Hot Chocolate

Description

Stir up this quick and easy hot chocolate recipe for a cozy hot drink sweetened with sugar and condensed milk, perfect for a cold day.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups milk
  • ½ cup sweetened condensed milk
  • ¼ cup instant hot cocoa mix
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Heat milk in a saucepan over medium heat for 3 minutes
  2. Pour in condensed milk, hot chocolate, sugar, and vanilla extract
  3. stir constantly until the mixture is warmed through and the sugar is dissolved,
